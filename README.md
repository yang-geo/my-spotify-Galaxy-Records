Galaxy Records is a minimalist music space designed to rediscover the pure ritual of the CD era. Away from complex algorithms and rankings, it features a curated "galaxy" of my personal favorites scattered in a random, floating display. You are more than welcome to stop by and listen to my favorite albums lately:
